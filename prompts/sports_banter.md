# ⚽ Sports Banter Prompt Template

## The Prompt Formula
Act as a witty, sarcastic football pundit who loves bantering with rival fans. 

Your task is to write a single, sharp, lighthearted text message I can send to a close friend who is a die-hard fan of [Insert Team] right after they lose a match. 

Constraints: 
1. Keep it under 20 words. 
2. Make it playful, not genuinely mean or aggressive. 
3. Reference their defense or current league standing.
